Financial Privacy Through Technology

The Zcash Foundation is a 501(c)3 nonprofit. Our mission is to build and support technology infrastructure that enables people to control their own financial data.

The Foundation sees the protocol Zcash, with its landmark use of zero-knowledge cryptography, as the most promising long-term approach.
